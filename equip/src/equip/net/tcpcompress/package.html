A plug-in to provide compression of communication (currently only over TCP} with {@link equip.net.ConnectionSap}.

Requires jzlib 1.0.1 (or above) in CLASSPATH - see http://www.jcraft.com/jzlib/. There may be a copy of the jar in CVS.

Requires configuration via {@link equip.config.ConfigManager} to have been read prior to use, e.g. in your config file: 

DefaultTcpFilter: equip.net.tcpcompress.JzlibFilterFactory
DefaultTcpFilterArg: 1
JzlibDisableNagle: y
JzlibTCPSendBufSize: 5000
